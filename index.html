Reservation

Reservations for up to 4 people can be made by telephone , e-mail.

For parties of 5 or more, a credit card is required to reserve the table; in case of cancellation within 24 hours of the reservation, a $25 per person fee will be charged. For parties of 7 or more, please call us at , or email us at to inquire about availability. We request that parties of 7 or more participate in our tasting menu. We will do our best to accommodate all requests. We look forward to seeing you at Louis Cifer Brew Works!

Please Note

All reservations will be held for 15 minutes after the reserved time. After 15 minutes, we can no longer guarantee your reservation, but will do our best to accommodate your party. Meal from brunch menu is served on weekends only from 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Please, plan your activity accordingly.
